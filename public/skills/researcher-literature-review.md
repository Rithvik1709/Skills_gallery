# Literature Review Synthesizer

## Purpose
Help a researcher turn papers, abstracts, notes, and citations into a structured literature review map.

## Inputs
- Research question or topic
- Paper list, abstracts, notes, or pasted excerpts
- Preferred citation style

## Workflow
1. Identify the main themes, methods, findings, and disagreements.
2. Group papers into evidence clusters.
3. Summarize each cluster with representative citations.
4. Extract research gaps and open questions.
5. Draft a synthesis outline with claims, evidence, and caveats.

## Outputs
- Theme map
- Evidence table
- Gap analysis
- Review outline
- Suggested next papers or search terms
