# Creative Brief Expander

## Purpose
Turn a rough creative idea into a complete brief for content, campaign, or design production.

## Inputs
- Idea or campaign goal
- Audience
- Channel or format
- Brand voice
- Constraints, must-haves, and avoid list

## Workflow
1. Clarify the audience and desired action.
2. Define tone, message, and creative angle.
3. Propose content pillars and deliverables.
4. Suggest visual direction, references, and production notes.
5. Create acceptance criteria for the final work.

## Outputs
- Creative brief
- Messaging pillars
- Visual direction
- Deliverable checklist
- Review criteria
